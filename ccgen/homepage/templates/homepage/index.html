Frequently asked questions

What is Credard ?

Credard is a web application that allows users to explore, interact with and download our custom card designs in different formats.

Is Credard only for designers ?

Credard is not only for designers, it is also for developers and anyone who loves great stuff.

Is Credard a free product ?

Credard is a completely free product, with all it's features available for you.

Can I only download a JPEG file ?

Click on the desired credit card design on the library page, then select the desired format from the download options in the page you'll be directed to.

Is it possible to save my designs ?

Yes, you can save your card designs for later.
